## Hero Service: The AI Readiness Audit

A structured, professional audit of a business's current operations — identifying where AI can save the most time and money, and what to build first. Not a generic report. A business-specific roadmap built from real interviews.

**Step 1 — Discovery Interviews:** 15–30 minute AI readiness interviews with the business owner and key team members covering daily tasks, biggest time drains, frustration levels, and what "good" would look like.

**Step 2 — Analysis:** Patterns across the team: where time is being lost, where systems are broken, where AI can have immediate ROI, and where it would be wasted effort.

**Step 3 — The Report:** Each team member receives an individual AI Readiness Report (executive summary, quantified time lost, prioritised recommendations, ROI projections). The business owner receives a consolidated report with organisation-wide findings, prioritised implementation roadmap, investment estimates, and quick-win vs. long-term breakdown.

**Step 4 — Presentation:** Findings presented to the leadership team.

**Pricing:** Entry-level from $1,500 USD. Multi-team enterprise audits quoted on application.

**Free entry point:** The AI Profit Wizard at audit.clearlightai.com — a free online audit tool. Business owners self-assess in 10–15 minutes and receive an instant report.

## Case Studies

### Spartans Gym & Fitness (Australia)
**Engagement:** AI Readiness Audit — CEO + 5 department managers

Danny Vorhauer, CEO of a multi-entity conglomerate (35,000 sq ft facility), was working 15–18 hour days, 7 days a week, handling 200+ daily communications. Only 3–5 hours/week on genuine strategic work. His managers were each losing 20–50% of their week to manual tasks.

**Key findings across six audited team members:**
- CEO: 4–6 hours/day on email/message triage, waking at 3:30 AM to physically walk through the gym
- One manager: 30% of her week lost to "information detective work" between disconnected systems
- One manager: 10 years of institutional knowledge trapped in a personal OneNote file
- One manager: only 33% of her time (5 hours/week) on high-value work

**Result:** 25+ hours/week of recoverable time identified across the team. Phase 1 recommendation: Manager Support Agent to reduce CEO's inbound communication volume.

### Human Garage / Centred
**Engagement:** AI Lead Scoring System — 760 contacts analysed

High-ticket certification program with hundreds of inbound applications per month. Sales team manually reviewing each one.

**Key findings:**
- Financial readiness is the #1 conversion predictor: 53% conversion with funds available vs. 2% with tight/no funding — a 26x difference
- Urgency is NOT reliable: zero of 54 "ASAP" applicants with tight funding ever converted
- Score 26+: 52% historical conversion rate. Score below 15: 0% conversion — zero buyers ever scored this low
- Winners wrote 58 words on average in applications; losers wrote 29
- **No winner ever scored Low. The system has never missed a real buyer.**
- Cost to operate: ~$0.01 per lead ($2–6/month)
