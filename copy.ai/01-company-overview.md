## Founders

### Russell Price — Founder & Director
Russell comes from a professional background in ghostwriting and editing, with credits at major publishing houses. He transitioned into AI consulting after seeing how much time and money small business owners were losing to manual, repetitive work. He bridges high-quality communication with practical AI implementation - meaning clients get solutions that are clear, well-documented, and actually used.

### Stephen Torrence — Co-Founder & CTO
Stephen's background spans technical systems and community-building practices. He brings the engineering rigour to every build, managing integrations, infrastructure, and the technical architecture of client systems.

Together, Russell and Stephen cover the full delivery stack: strategy, communication, and implementation.

## What We Do (One Sentence)

We help entrepreneurs, coaches, and small business owners find where AI will actually save them time and money, then build the systems to make it happen.

---

## Mission

Most business owners know AI is important. Very few know where to start, what's worth building, and what's just hype. ClearLight AI exists to close that gap - with honest assessment, concrete recommendations, and hands-on implementation from people who do this every day.

---

## Target Clients

- **Entrepreneurs and solopreneurs** running service-based businesses (coaching, consulting, wellness, professional services)
- **SMB owners** with small to mid-size teams (5-50 people) who have operational pain but no internal tech team
- **Coaches and course creators** wanting to automate delivery, lead management, and client communication
- **Executives and business leaders** drowning in communication volume who need intelligent support agents

Common sectors: fitness/wellness, healthcare, professional services, online education, property, hospitality.

---

## How We're Different

Most AI vendors sell you a tool. We audit your business first.

We interview your team, map your workflows, identify the highest-ROI automation opportunities, and give you a prioritised implementation roadmap. Then we build it.

We are not a software company. We are not a generalist marketing agency. We are implementation specialists - small, focused, and accountable.

---

## Brand Tone

Direct. Honest. Practical. No hype, no jargon, no overpromising. We speak to business owners, not tech teams.
