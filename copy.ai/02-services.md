## Hero Service: The AI Readiness Audit

This is our flagship entry-point offer and the primary focus of the landing page.

### What It Is

A structured, professional audit of a business's current operations - identifying where AI can save the most time and money, and what to build first. It is not a generic report. It is a business-specific roadmap built from real interviews with the business owner and their team.

### How It Works

**Step 1 - Discovery Interviews**
We conduct 15-30 minute AI readiness interviews with the business owner and key team members. Each person walks us through their daily tasks, their biggest time drains, their frustration levels, and what "good" would look like if those problems were solved.

**Step 2 - Analysis**
We analyse patterns across the team: where time is being lost, where systems are broken, where AI can have immediate ROI, and where it would be wasted effort.

**Step 3 - The Report**
Each team member receives an individual AI Readiness Report covering:
- Executive summary of their role and pain points
- Quantified time lost to manual/repetitive tasks
- Prioritised AI automation recommendations
- Expected time savings and ROI projections

The business owner or CEO receives a consolidated report with:
- Organisation-wide findings
- Prioritised implementation roadmap (Phase 1, Phase 2, etc.)
- Investment estimates for each phase
- Quick-win vs. long-term build breakdown

**Step 4 - Presentation**
We present findings to the leadership team and answer questions.

### What Clients Get

- Individual readiness report for each staff member interviewed
- Master findings report with implementation roadmap
- Phase 1 recommendation (the highest-ROI first build)
- A clear, documented brief for implementation - whether with us or their own team

### Who It's For

- Business owners who know AI is relevant but don't know where to start
- Operations leaders who are drowning in manual tasks and communication volume
- Entrepreneurs preparing to scale and wanting to automate before hiring
- Executives with teams of 3-50 people

### Pricing

Priced by scope (number of team members audited and complexity of the business). Entry-level from $1,500 USD. Multi-team enterprise audits quoted on application.

### Free Entry Point

The **AI Profit Wizard** at audit.clearlightai.com is a free online audit tool. Business owners can self-assess in 10-15 minutes and receive an instant report. This is the primary lead generation tool.

## Additional Services

Once a client has completed an audit (with us or independently), implementation services are available.

---

### Company Brain (RAG Knowledge Agent)

A custom AI assistant built on the business's own documents, SOPs, policies, and knowledge base. Staff or customers can ask it questions and get accurate, sourced answers in seconds.

**Built for:**
- Internal knowledge management (replacing the "human wiki")
- Customer support automation
- Onboarding and training support

**Technology stack:** RAG (Retrieval Augmented Generation), OpenWebUI, custom vector databases.

**Example use case:** Spartans Gym team members were losing hours per week answering repetitive internal questions. A Company Brain trained on their SOPs, policy documents, and operational data would handle 80%+ of those queries automatically.

---

### AI Executive / Chief of Staff Agent

A custom AI agent that sits across a CEO or executive's communication stack - synthesising messages, triaging email and Slack, drafting responses for approval, and generating weekly reports.

**Built for:**
- Executives handling 100-200+ messages per day
- Multi-entity business owners who are the primary bottleneck
- Leaders who need to reclaim strategic thinking time

**Technology stack:** LLM-based agents, Gmail/Slack integration, n8n automation pipelines.

---

### Lead Scoring System

An automated AI system that scores inbound leads the moment they submit an application or enquiry form - instantly categorising them as High / Medium / Low priority based on behavioural and demographic signals drawn from historical conversion data.

**What it delivers:**
- Automated score written to the CRM within 20 seconds of form submission
- AI-written lead summary for the sales rep
- Suggested personalised opening line for each call
- Score breakdown showing where the lead is strong or weak

**Technology stack:** LLM scoring model, GoHighLevel or equivalent CRM, n8n workflow automation.

**Cost to operate:** Approximately $0.01 per lead. At typical volumes, $2-6/month.

---

### Voice Agent

A custom AI voice agent built with ElevenLabs and integrated into a website or chat system. Sounds like a real human. Can be a clone of the business owner's voice or a custom persona.

**Built for:**
- Coaches, course creators, and personal brands wanting 24/7 interactive presence
- Lead qualification and FAQ handling
- Membership communities and private learning portals

---

### Content Creation Agent

A custom AI content system trained on the client's voice, style, and historical content. Produces drafts for email, social media, and long-form content that actually sound like the client.

**Built for:**
- Coaches and course creators drowning in content demands
- Personal brands with regular publishing requirements
- Teams running multi-channel campaigns

---

### OpenWebUI / Private AI Deployment

Deployment of a private, self-hosted AI platform for businesses that want their own ChatGPT-style interface with custom models, tools, and user access control - without data going to third-party servers.

**Built for:**
- Businesses with sensitive client data
- Teams wanting a single AI interface for multiple use cases
- Clients who want control over model selection and costs

**Infrastructure:** AWS, Docker, OpenRouter model routing.

---

### Automation Pipelines (n8n)

Custom automation workflows connecting tools, moving data, and eliminating manual steps across a business's software stack.

**Common use cases:**
- CRM data enrichment (automatically enriching contact records from web sources)
- Call transcript processing (auto-saving and summarising meeting recordings)
- Report generation (pulling data from multiple sources into a single weekly summary)
- Form-to-CRM workflows with conditional logic
