## Primary Design Brief

**Goal:** Convert business owners (entrepreneurs, SMB owners, coaches) into either:
1. Free audit completions at audit.clearlightai.com
2. Discovery call bookings

**Hero service:** The AI Readiness Audit - a paid, structured business audit that identifies the highest-ROI AI opportunities in a business and produces a professional, phased implementation roadmap.

**Tone:** Direct, credible, honest. Not hype-driven. Not corporate. We talk like a senior consultant who has done this many times and doesn't need to oversell.

**Visual direction:** Professional and modern. Navy, blue, and purple palette with gold accents. Clean layout. Data-forward where possible - the proof points are strong and should be visible.
