## Objection Handling

These are the most common reasons prospects hesitate. The landing page should address them proactively.

### "I don't know if AI is relevant to my business."
**Response:** That's exactly what the audit is for. We map your actual workflows, identify your real pain points, and tell you honestly what's worth building and what isn't. If AI won't move the needle for you, we'll tell you that.

### "I've tried AI tools before and they didn't work."
**Response:** Most AI tools are generic. They're not built around your business, your data, or your workflows. What we build is custom - trained on your documents, integrated with your existing tools, and designed to solve a specific problem you've told us about.

### "It's too expensive."
**Response:** The audit pays for itself. In almost every engagement, we identify recoverable time equivalent to a part-time or full-time salary. The question isn't whether you can afford to audit - it's whether you can afford to keep running the way you currently are.

### "I'm not technical."
**Response:** You don't need to be. We handle the build. You tell us what's painful and what good would look like. We do the rest. All systems are delivered with documentation and training so your team can run them without us.

### "I don't have time to go through a big process."
**Response:** The audit interviews are 15-30 minutes per team member. We do the analysis. You get the report. The whole process from first call to final report is typically 2-3 weeks.

## Page Structure Suggestion

The following section order is recommended for the landing page. Adjust based on design direction:

1. Hero - headline, subheadline, primary CTA (free audit)
2. Pain section - who this is for, what problems we solve
3. How it works - the audit process in 3-4 steps
4. Proof points / stats (Human Garage and Spartans data)
5. Services overview (audit as hero, other services as secondary)
6. Case studies / examples (2-3 featured)
7. Testimonials / social proof
8. About the founders
9. FAQs (objection handling)
10. Final CTA (book a call)
11. Footer with logo wall and links
